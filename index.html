Octogamut

The Octogamut is a retunable webaudio keyboard

Play the Octogamut with your keyboard or your mouse. Each key is labelled with its keyboard shortcut. Retune the Octogamut by entering a values in Hz below each key. They will update right away. Change the volume from 0 to 10 using the gain control. The default tuning is Subharmonics 14-07. A few more tunings are listed below, plus links to discover many more!
